Missing You

the great chasm, mighty! uninterrupted across the circumference. the distances as between blackholes at the center of galaxies. a void, filled with emptiness. with darkness so thick you can almost cut into it. such it is, the reality i stand in. my heart reaped from my cavity, held in situ, what feels like light years away. infinity could fit between the great divide that separates. this distance this curse. this only reason i am not in your arms. i despise it loth it hate it with a passion.

but there is hope. what starts as a flicker, an amber but as though a sun is birthed, explodes in the chasm. the void filled with light the knowledge that you are mine. the memories of your smile, the heat your body radiates.

i await, with patience like no other, to have you in my arms. to taste your lips. to witness your beautiful, so radiant. to have your company. and let your laugh bless my ears

i count down the days, hours, minutes down to the second, till mine eyes will gaze upon you. where words fail me, I simply stare and imagine life with you

oh what a life.

i miss you, beyond measure beyond words i long to be with you, to be near you to be able to lay my eyes on you always admiring all that which is mine.

oh what a life.
